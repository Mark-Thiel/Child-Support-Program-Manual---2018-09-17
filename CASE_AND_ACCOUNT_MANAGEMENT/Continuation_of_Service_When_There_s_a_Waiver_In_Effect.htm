Continuation of Service When There's a Waiver In Effect

If the TA Unit closed their case less than 30 days ago

If the customer wishes to apply for services as a private case after their temporary assistance case has been closed by the TA Unit, the customer does not need to fill out a new LDSS-4882. Instead, they may revise the previously submitted LDSS-4882.

To do this, the applicant must:

      1. Check Box 1 in Section C â€“ Application/Affirmation for Child Support Services;

      2. Complete the associated signature area; and

      3. Initial the changes to mark the LDSS-4882 as having been amended

If the TA Unit closed their case more than 30 days ago

If temporary assistance is denied and it has been more than 30 days since the customer signed the LDSS-4882, the customer must fill out a new LDSS-4882 and reapply for child support services, this time as a Non-TA ("private") case rather than a TA case.
