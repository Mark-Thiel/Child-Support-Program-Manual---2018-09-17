Does any party object to the COLA?

As noted in the "Adjusted Order of Support", either party or the SCU has thirty-five (35) days from the date of mailing of the adjusted order to file a written objection with the court and to serve a copy upon the other party and the SCU.

Any party (even the one who requested the COLA) can file an objection to a COLA.

If any party objects to a COLA, the COLA will not take effect.

An objection must be filed directly with the court.
