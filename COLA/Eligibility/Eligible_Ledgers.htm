Eligible Ledgers

Any given account may contain some ledgers that are subject to COLA increases and some ledgers that are not.

ASSETS displays on the Worker Alerts > COLA tab screen all accounts that contain COLA-eligible ledgers, even if an account contains a mix of eligible and ineligible ledgers.

There are a total of 11 types of ledgers that are eligible for COLA increases. They are:

Eligibility

11B_

Eligible if it has a status of 01

Eligible if it has a status of 01

24CZ

24CZ is eligible only if there is also an eligible 11B_ ledger

Ineligible Ledgers

When an account contains some eligible and some ineligible ledgers, the ineligible ledgers do not render the entirety of the account ineligible for a COLA increase. However, only the eligible ledgers will be factored in to the calculations of the COLA.
