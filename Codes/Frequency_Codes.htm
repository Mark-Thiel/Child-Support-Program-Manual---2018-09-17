Frequency Codes

Helpful tip:  Looking for a specific Frequency Code? From anywhere in this manual, just put the code into the Search box and hit Enter.  (Example: D02 + Enter, or M07 + Enter)

Code

Meaning

A

Annual payment; payment due 12 months after first payment

B

Semi-monthly, on the 1st and 15th of each month

D07

Payment due every seven (7) days

D14

Payment due every fourteen (14) days

DXX

Where XX = number of days  (e.g. D13 = every 13 days, D26 = every 26 days)

MXX

Monthly payment, where XX = the date of each month  (e.g. M17 = on the 17th day of each month, M24 = on the 24th day of each month)

Q

Quarterly payment, due on the first automated support collection unit (ASCU) day of the quarter

S

Semi-Annual; payment due 6 months after first payment
