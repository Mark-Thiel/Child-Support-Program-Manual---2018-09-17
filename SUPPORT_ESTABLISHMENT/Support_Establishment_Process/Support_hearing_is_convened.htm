Support hearing is convened

If the NCP fails to appear for the hearing

  • If the summons was mailed to the NCP and he/she fails to appear for the hearing, the court will adjourn for personal service of a (second) summons.

  • If the NCP was personally served a summons and fails to appear at the hearing, the Support Magistrate can issue a default order.

See also:  Summons -- Support

If the CP fails to appear for the hearing

The Support Petition can be dismissed.
