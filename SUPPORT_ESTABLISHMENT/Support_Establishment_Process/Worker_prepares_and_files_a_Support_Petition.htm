  1. What is a Support Petition?

    A Support Petition is a document filed in family court requesting that the court issue an Order of Support.

  2. When to File a Support Petition

    Source:  ASSETS User Guide

    You must file a Support Petition when the following conditions have been met:

    This form is generated through the Document Generation module in ASSETS.

    Support Petition (Individual) (Non-TA cases)  (sample)

    This form is generated through the Document Generation module in ASSETS.

      • You have a verified address for the NCP

    (This means that there is a Code V, Code R, or Code M in the Address Verification Ind field.)

    AND

      • Paternity has been established

    Also, technologically, the following conditions must be met in order to generate a Support Petition:

     There is a child record with a child under 21 years of age

     The Wedlock Indicator is 1, 2, A, or H

     The Family Violence Indicator is not 1 or F

    If the petition is filed on paper, the CSEU must also send a Personal Information Form to the court.

    Important: Both parties will receive copies of any and all attachments to the Support Petition. Therefore, in the interest of confidentiality, the Personal Information Form should not be attached to the Support Petition, but rather should be sent to court separately per local protocol.

  1. How to E-File a Support Petition

    During the e-filing process, the case will be assigned a court hearing date.

    For an explanation of the entire process for e-filing paternity and support petitions — as well as FAQs and details about implementation —  see E-Filing Petitions in ASSETS, a 36-page manual located at http://otda.state.nyenet/assets/pages/PDFs/Systems_-_ASSETS/E-Filing_Petitions_in_ASSETS.pdf

  2. Receiving Notification of a Hearing Date

    After the Support Petition is filed, the CSEU will be notified of a hearing date according to local protocols. (The notification may come by mail, fax, email, courier, or some other method.)

  3. Check the Status of a Support Petition/Request for a Hearing

    Within an individual case record, workers can check the Summary Mode > Court Information tab to see the court date for the case, if one has been scheduled.
