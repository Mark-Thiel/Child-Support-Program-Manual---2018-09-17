Support Magistrate continues the paternity hearing to a support hearing

A Support Magistrate is a court officer who presides over family court hearings and has jurisdiction over:

Once paternity is adjudicated at a paternity hearing, the Support Magistrate may continue the hearing and issue an Order of Support. In other words, paternity is established, support is established, and an Order of Support is issued, all at the same hearing, without having to file a separate Support Petition.
