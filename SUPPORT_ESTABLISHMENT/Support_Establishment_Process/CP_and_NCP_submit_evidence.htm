  1. The CP or his/her representative submits evidence to court

    The CP must submit the following to court:

      1. Address Confidentiality Affidavit, if applicable

      2. Affidavit of Service, if applicable (proving that the NCP was served a summons)

      3. Child's birth certificate

      4. Marriage license or divorce decree, if applicable

      5. Acknowledgment of Paternity, if applicable

      6. Financial Disclosure Affidavit (notarized), with the following attached:

          • Proof of public assistance (if applicable)

          • Two (2) most recent pay stubs

          • Most recent Federal and state tax returns

          • W-2s and/or 1099 statements

          • All documents which prove the amount of other income and/or debt and loans

          • Proof of health insurance coverage and cost

    If either party has health insurance available through employment, but has not yet signed up for it, they must bring proof of the coverage and cost.

        • Evidence regarding living expenses and/or expenses related to special needs (medical, educational, etc.) of the child, which may include:

              1. Rent

              2. Food

              3. Clothing

              4. Child care

              5. Medical insurance

              6. Education for the child

  2. The NCP or his/her representative submits evidence to court

    The NCP must bring the following documents to court:

      1. Financial Disclosure Affidavit (notarized), with the following attached:

        • Two most recent pay stubs

        • Most recent federal and State tax returns

        • W-2s and/or 1099 statements

        • Proof of health insurance coverage and its cost

        • Social Security statement, if applicable

        • Proof of public assistance, if applicable

        • Any other Support Orders (for other children, not on this case) of the NCP

        • Receipts for any child support already paid in relation to this case
