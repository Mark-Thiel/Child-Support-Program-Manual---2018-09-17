Does the customer seek child support?

In some courts, once paternity is established at a paternity hearing, the court/Support Magistrate will seek to establish a support order at that same hearing.

The CSEU cannot establish a support order without a signed request/statement from the customer requesting child support.

Converting a Paternity Petition to a Support Petition

If a court wishes to amend a Paternity Petition to include a request for child support services, the customer/CP must file an Addendum to Support Petition, or an approved equivalent, or petition must be amended with language to include request for support services.

Even if the court issues a support order, the CSEU cannot establish a support order with a written and signed statement from the customer requesting child support services.

When the CSEU has a location for a PF/NCP, and paternity has been established, the worker can prepare a Support Petition. If paternity still needs to be established, the worker should instead prepare a Paternity Petition.
