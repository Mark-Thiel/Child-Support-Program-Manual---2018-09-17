Support Magistrate converts the Paternity Hearing to a Support Hearing

In some courts, when paternity is adjudicated during a paternity hearing, the Support Magistrate will then proceed —  at that same hearing — to issue an Order of Support.  

If this happens:

 

  1. The CP or his/her representative will file an Addendum to Support Petition.

The Addendum to Support Petition serves — for the purposes of the court  — as a request (i.e. petition) for child support services.
