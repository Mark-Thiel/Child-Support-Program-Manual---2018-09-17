Change of Payee — TA Cases

Source:  Notes provided by Monroe County - DHS Cases

The Child Support Program allows CPs with direct pay orders to have the orders made payable through the CSEU administratively — without having to petition the family court. This is known as a "Change of Payee" (procedure).

Once the order is payable through the CSEU, the order is subject to all enforcement and collection tools available to the CSEU.

Why Is the Payee Being Changed?

A Change of Payee (TA cases) scenario is one in which:

    1. The customer and the NCP already had a direct pay order between them.

    2. The customer then applied for temporary assistance.

    3. TA applicants/recipients are required to cooperate with the Child Support Program as a condition of receiving temporary assistance. Therefore, the direct-pay order must be made payable to DSS through the CSEU.
