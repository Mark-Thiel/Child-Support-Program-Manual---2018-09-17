Change of Payee — Non-TA Cases

Source:  Notes provided by Monroe County - DHS Cases

The Child Support Program allows CPs who have direct pay orders to have orders made payable through the CSEU (through a "Change of Payee" procedure) administratively —  meaning, without having to petition the family court.

The customer must complete and sign an LDSS-4882.

Once the order is made payable through the CSEU, the order is subject to all enforcement and collection tools available to the CSEU.
