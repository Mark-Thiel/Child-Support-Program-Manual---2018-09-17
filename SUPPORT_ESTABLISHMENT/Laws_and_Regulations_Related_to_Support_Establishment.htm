Laws and Regulations Related to Support Establishment

Source: CS101 Manual

The following laws and regulations provide the legal framework for child support orders to ensure that each order is fair, reasonable, and uniform for all child support cases.

Description

Provides the legal framework requiring parents to provide financial support for their children until age 21 and defines terms and phrases relevant to support establishment.

Provides legal framework for the provision of health insurance benefits by either or both parents.

Child Support Standards Act

NYS law that provides the legal framework for a formula to calculate the amount of child support each that the noncustodial parent must pay; it also dictates how much each the noncustodial parent is ordered pay for other items such as medical, educational, and child care expenses.
