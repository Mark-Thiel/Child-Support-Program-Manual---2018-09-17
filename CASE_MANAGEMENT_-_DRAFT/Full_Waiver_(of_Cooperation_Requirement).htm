Full Waiver (of Cooperation Requirement)

Source:  03-ADM-05

Full or partial waivers pertain to TA cases only.

A waiver can exempt a customer in whole or in part from the requirement to cooperate with the CSEU as a condition of receiving temporary assistance.

If a Full Waiver has been granted, the CSEU will be notified of this by the DVL, via form LDSS-2859.

If a full waiver has been granted, all enforcement actions must be stopped.  Any arrears will continue to accrue.

Note that if a full waiver has been granted, but the customer still wishes to pursue an order, they can do so.
