Introduction to Account Creation

Once the court issues an Order of Support, the information contained in it must be entered into the case record, and an account and ledgers must be created. Ledgers are sometimes referred to as "sub-accounts."

The following is a summary of the steps involved in creating an account:

    1. Retrieve the Account Creation Summary (ACS) and/or the Order of Support from the Family Court Interface > Reports tab

    2. Extract needed information from the ACS or Order of Support

    3. Enter that information into form DSS-3117

    4. Build an account, using the information contained in form DSS-3117

Note: This is typically done by a different person from the one who entered the information into the DSS-3117 in the previous step.
