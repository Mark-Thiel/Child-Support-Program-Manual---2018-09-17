What is the DSS-3117?

The DSS-3117 is a State-approved paper form used to transpose and organize information in an Order of Support in preparation for that information being entered into the case record/account.

One worker reads the Order of Support, using the information in it to fill out the DSS-3117.

A different worker then reads the DSS-3117, using the information in it to build a new account (or update an existing one).

The purpose of this is separation of duties.
