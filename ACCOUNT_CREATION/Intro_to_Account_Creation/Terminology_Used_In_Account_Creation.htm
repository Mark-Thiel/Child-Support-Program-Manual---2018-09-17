Terminology Used In Account Creation

Source: Account Creation Participant Manual

ACS:  Account Creation Summary

Arrears:  The balance when the NCP has not paid the regular amounts of money that were due

Charges:  Recurring amounts becoming due

Current Support:  The balance of the regular recurring support

Due:  In arrears, outstanding, owed, owing, payable, unpaid

FIPS:  Federal Information Processing Standards Code

IWO:  Income Withholding Order

Ledgers:  Sub-accounts

Owed:  Obligated to pay

TA:  Temporary Assistance

* Arrears are done only through the court.  (Back child support is not the same thing as "arrears."

Judgment Ledgers  (all arrears are judgments.  Please confirm.)
