Worker provides LDSS-4882 to customer

Source:  12-ADM-03

The CSEU must provide the LDSS-4882 to anyone who requests one.  

If the request is made in person, the CSEU must provide the LDSS-4882 to the customer on the day of the request.

If the request is made by telephone or in writing, the CSEU must send the LDSS-4882 to the customer within five (5) business days of the request.

The CSEU should keep a log of dates when LDSS-4882 forms were provided to customers, and also enter such dates into the case records once the cases are built. This is important, as it relates to federal data reliability audits.

Note:  Customer Service Representatives (CSRs) at the New York State Child Support Helpline (CSH) (at the Processing Center) will send the LDSS-4882 to customers who request one, as well as inform them of the availability of the LDSS-4882 for print or download on the New York State child support website, at childsupport.ny.gov.
