Implementation of Revised Federal IWO/Notice for Support details changes to the IWO effective with the August 29 overnight production run.  The Federal Office of Child Support Enforcement (OCSE) issued a revised Income Withholding Order/Notice for Support (IWO).  OCSE now requires the issuer of the IWO to provide a specific withholding percentage, up to the Federal Consumer Credit Protection Act (CCPA) limits, in the correct data field to avoid over-withholding from a noncustodial parent.
