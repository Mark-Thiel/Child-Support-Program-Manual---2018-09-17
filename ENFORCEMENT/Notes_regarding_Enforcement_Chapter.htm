Notes regarding Enforcement Chapter

DMV

TRO

Court-based Methods of Enforcement:

    1. Money judgment for the amount past due

    2. Jail term of up to six months

    3. Probation

    4. DMV - Driver's license suspension (based upon a violation petition)

    5. Suspending a professional license

Administrative Methods of Enforcement:

    1. Driver's license suspension

    2. Property Execution (PEX)

    3. IEX - Income Withholding

    4. TAX - NYS Dept of Tax and Finance Referral

    5. Consumer Reporting Agency

    6. Lottery Prize Intercept

    7. Professional license suspension

    8. Passport Denial

    9. TRO - Tax Refund Offset

    10. United States Attorney's Office (USAO) Referral
