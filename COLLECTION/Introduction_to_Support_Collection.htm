Introduction to Support Collection

Source: 03 ADM 5

Once an order of support has been established in court, the CSEU builds/establishes an account reflecting the amount of the support obligation and the frequency of payments required by the order.

If we have a verified employer address for the NCP, ASSETS will automatically send an IWO to the NCP's employer. IWOs are sent to employers in an automated administrative process that deducts support payments from an NCP's wages or other income, including unemployment benefits.

If there is no verified employer address, an IWO cannot be issued.  Instead, a computer-generated billing statement is mailed to the NCP, advising the dates and amounts that scheduled payments are due.
