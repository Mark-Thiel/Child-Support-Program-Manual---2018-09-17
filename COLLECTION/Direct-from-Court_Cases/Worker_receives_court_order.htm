Worker receives court order

The Worker can take information to build the case and account from the court order or from the court interface.

The court order may come to the CSEU on paper, or it may be sent electronically, depending upon local DSS procedures.

The court order alone is not sufficient to build a case, since it does not serve as a request for child support services. To build a case, the CSEU must have a request for services, signed by the applicant, in the form of a petition, addendum, or motion. Without it, there is no request for services, and the CSEU is not authorized to initiate building of a case or account.
