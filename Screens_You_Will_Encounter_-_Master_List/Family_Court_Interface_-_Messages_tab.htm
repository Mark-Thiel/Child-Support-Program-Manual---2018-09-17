Family Court Interface > Messages tab

Modules > Court > Messages

The Family Court Interface pulls information from the database of the Office of Court Administration (OCA).  Copies of court orders are available through the interface.

Other information available through the Family Court Interface includes the following:

      1. Orders already established for the parties (court histories)

      2. Whether or not a previous Support Petition has been filed and/or dismissed by the court
