NOTES re Processing Center

According to emails from Juliet:

When documents are received at the Processing Center, they are sorted by document type.

All documents received at the Processing Center are imaged and archived.

All scanned documents undergo some OCR (optical character recognition).

All documents scanned into the Image Data Capture (IDC) are queued for manual data entry after passing through the OCR.

For some documents, the original of the document must be forwarded to the local districts.
