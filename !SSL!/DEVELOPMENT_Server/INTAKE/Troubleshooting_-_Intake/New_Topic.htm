Worker is kicked out of online Application Form

If the worker hits the "Backspace" key while entering information into the online version of the application/referral A request sent to a CSEU from another jurisdiction or a non-IV-D agent or agency asking that a child support order be established. form, the ASSETS Automated State Support Enforcement and Tracking System (ASSETS). system will kick the worker out of the application form.

Therefore, workers should:

    • Refrain from hitting the "backspace" key while filling in an online application.

    • Save their work after completing each section of the form.
