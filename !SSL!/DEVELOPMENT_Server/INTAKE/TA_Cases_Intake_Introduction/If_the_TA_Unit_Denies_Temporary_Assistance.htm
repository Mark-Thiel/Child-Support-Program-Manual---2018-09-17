If the TA Unit Denies a Temporary Assistance Application

If temporary assistance has been denied by the TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA). Unit less than 30 days prior and the customer wishes to apply for services as a private case Among child support workers, a "private case" is understood to mean a Non-TA case. In a private case, the customer is not mandated or required to cooperate with the child support program, but rather is applying for services of their own accord., they do not need to fill out a new LDSS-4882. Instead, they may revise the previously submitted LDSS-4882.

To do this, the applicant must:

      1. Check Box 1 in Section C – Application/Affirmation for Child Support Services.

      2. Complete the associated signature area.

      3. Initial the changes to mark the LDSS-4882 as having been amended.

If temporary assistance has been denied by the TA Unit more than 30 days prior, the customer must complete a new LDSS-4882 "Information about Child Support Services and "Application/Referral for Child Support Services" intake form. (Form LDSS-4882) — this time applying as a private case.
