Worker sends customer to DVL

If a customer indicates a safety concern on the LDSS-4882 "Information about Child Support Services and "Application/Referral for Child Support Services" intake form. (Form LDSS-4882), the worker must stop the intake interview and refer the customer — using locally developed procedures  —  to a Domestic Violence Liaison for screening and assessment.

The CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit must notify the TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA). Unit of this action via the Information Transmittal form, LDSS-2859.

All CSEU activities must be suspended during the domestic violence screening and assessment process and until a waiver decision is made by the Domestic Violence Liaison.
