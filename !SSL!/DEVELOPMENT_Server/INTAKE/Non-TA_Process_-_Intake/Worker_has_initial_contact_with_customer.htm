The worker must explain to the Non-TA Non-Temporary Assistance customer the full range of child support services, including:

    • Which services are non-optional when a customer applies for child support services.

    • Which services are optional.

    • What services are not provided by the CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit.

Basic (Non-Optional) Services, provided for all cases:

The following are services provided to all Non-TA customers applying for child support services:

      1. Collection of child support payments through the Support Collection Unit.

(All payments must go through the CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit.)

      1. Enforcement of support obligations through all available means.

(Customers cannot choose which enforcement actions will be pursued and which won't.)

All of the above services are provided for a single $25 annual fee.  (Click here to see a six-minute video explaining the fee):

The worker should ask the customer to provide specific information needed to carry out actions necessary for her/his case (e.g. paternity establishment, support establishment, enforcement).
