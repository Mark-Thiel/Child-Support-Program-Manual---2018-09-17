CP or NCP requests legal services

Source:  12-ADM-03

If the Non-TA Non-Temporary Assistance customer is the CP Custodial Parent or Custodial Party. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child., and wishes to apply for legal services, he or she must indicate this by checking Box 1 in Section C â€“ Application/Affirmation for Child Support Services on the LDSS-4882.

Either the CP or NCP can request legal services at any time that there is an open child support case.

(Note:  Customers in foster care, TA, or Medicaid cases are not given this form, since the DSS Department of Social Services does not seek cost recovery for legal services provided to these customers.)
