To the Customer in TA Cases Involving Domestic Violence:

03-ADM-05 provides an attachment addressed directly to the customer in TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA). cases who is considering requesting a domestic violence waiver.

It is located at: http://otda.ny.gov/policy/directives/2006/INF/03-ADM-05-attach.pdf, and can be printed out and given to the customer.

The document begins:

 

NOTICE REGARDING CHILD SUPPORT REQUIREMENTS FOR

VICTIMS OF DOMESTIC VIOLENCE

"If you are a Temporary Assistance (TA) client and a victim of domestic violence (DV Domestic Violence.), please carefully read the following information about Child Support Enforcement (CSE Child Support Enforcement) services before you request a waiver from the child support requirement..."
