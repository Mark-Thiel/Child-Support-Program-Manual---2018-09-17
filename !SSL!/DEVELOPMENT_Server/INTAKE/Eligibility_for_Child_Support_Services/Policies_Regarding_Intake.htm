Equal Access to Services

Non-TA Non-Temporary Assistance cases are entitled to receive, on an equal basis, all child support services that are available for TA cases.  

Non-TA applicants are to be advised of:

    1.  All services available to them (location, paternity establishment, support establishment, collection, and enforcement)

    2.  The distribution The allocation of child support collected to the various types of debt (e.g., monthly support obligations, arrears, ordered arrears) within a child support case as specified in 45 CFR 302.51 (45 CFR 309.115 for procedures governing tribal child support programs); the process of how the total child support payment amount is divided between all those owed under the support orders, including reimbursement for public assistance. policy

    3.  The mandatory $25 annual federal CP Custodial Parent or Custodial Party. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child. fee (this applies to "never assistance cases" only)

    4.  Costs associated with any legal representation provided in Non-TA cases [18NYCRR 347.17]
