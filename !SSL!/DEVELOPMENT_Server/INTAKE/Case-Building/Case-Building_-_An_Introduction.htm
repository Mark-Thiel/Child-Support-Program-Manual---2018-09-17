If the Paper LDSS-4882 Has Not Arrived at the CSEU

    • If the CSEU has not yet received a paper LDSS-4882 when the referral appears on the IVA-IVD Interface Report, the CSEU should notify the TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA). Unit of this via an LDSS-2859.

    • If the CSEU still has not received an LDSS-4882 ten (10) days after the referral appears on the IVA-IVD Interface Report, the worker should:

        1. Notify the TA Unit, via an LDSS-2859, that the CSEU still has not received an LDSS-4882.

        2. Reach out to the customer and try to schedule an interview.

Using Referral Search to Build the Case

Why should you build the case through Referral Search?

Because in doing that, a link between WMS Welfare Management System. The WMS system used by the TA units supplies referrals and other information to the ASSETS system used by Child Support units.. The relationship is one-way only: WMS can update some information in the ASSETS system (CP residential addresses, for example), but the ASSETS system cannot update any datafields in the WMS system. and ASSETS Automated State Support Enforcement and Tracking System (ASSETS). will be created for the case, and information about the CP Custodial Parent or Custodial Party. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child. and Child(ren) will be automatically imported from WMS into ASSETS.
