Continuation of Service When There's a Waiver In Effect

If the TA Unit closed their case less than 30 days ago

If the customer wishes to apply for services as a private case Among child support workers, a "private case" is understood to mean a Non-TA case. In a private case, the customer is not mandated or required to cooperate with the child support program, but rather is applying for services of their own accord. after their temporary assistance case has been closed by the TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA). Unit, the customer does not need to fill out a new LDSS-4882. Instead, they may revise the previously submitted LDSS-4882.

If the TA Unit closed their case more than 30 days ago

If temporary assistance is denied and it has been more than 30 days since the customer signed the LDSS-4882 "Information about Child Support Services and "Application/Referral for Child Support Services" intake form. (Form LDSS-4882), the customer must fill out a new LDSS-4882 and reapply for child support services, this time as a Non-TA ("private") case rather than a TA case.
