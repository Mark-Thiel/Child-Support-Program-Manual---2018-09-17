Court receives objection and notifies the SCU

The court receives the objection A written request that a Judge review a decision of a Support Magistrate. from the CP Custodial Parent or Custodial Party. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child. or NCP.

The Court Clerk sends notification of the objection to the SCU Support Collection Unit..

The notification will include the official Objection Date.
