If the objection is upheld

If the objection A written request that a Judge review a decision of a Support Magistrate. to the COLA Cost-of-Living Adjustment, sometimes called a Cost-of-Living Allowance. is upheld, the Adjusted Order of Support issued to the CP Custodial Parent or Custodial Party. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child. and NCP must be rescinded:

    • The worker clears the Adj. Order Objection Date field.

    • The worker rescinds the Adjusted Order of Support by entering Code "R" (rescind Revoke, cancel, or repeal (a law, order, or agreement).) in the COLA Request Indicator field on the Cost of Living Adjustment (COLA) Information Edit screen.

The Court Order Date is the earlier of the date of the court determination on the Obligation Tab screen or the Adj Order Effective Date on the COLA Tab screen (i.e., 60 days from the date the adjusted order was mailed).
