If the objection is dismissed

If the objection A written request that a Judge review a decision of a Support Magistrate. to the proposed COLA Cost-of-Living Adjustment, sometimes called a Cost-of-Living Allowance. is dismissed by the court:

    • The worker clears the Adj. Order Objection Date field, allowing the COLA process to continue.

    • The Adjusted Order of Support is processed (by ASSETS Automated State Support Enforcement and Tracking System (ASSETS).) as planned, and takes effect on the Adj. Order Effective Date).
