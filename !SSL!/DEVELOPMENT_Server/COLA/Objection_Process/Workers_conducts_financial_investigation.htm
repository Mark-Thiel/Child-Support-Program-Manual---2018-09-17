Worker prepares for court and conducts financial investigation

The worker conducts a financial investigation as would otherwise be undertaken in a case to establish an original support order A court order that decides the how much money should be paid for the support of a child or spouse (husband or wife). A support order can include money; health care; payment of debts; or repayment of court costs and attorney fees, interest, and penalties; and other kinds of support., and provides the court with such financial information as is available on ASSETS Automated State Support Enforcement and Tracking System (ASSETS)..
