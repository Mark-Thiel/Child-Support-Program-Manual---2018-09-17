Family Court Act 413 and Domestic Relations Law 240 Section 1-b, the law that governs child support in New York. Known as the CSSA, or simply "the guidelines," it defines a basic child support obligation by percentages of income, i.e., 17% of combined parental income for one child, 25% for two children, 29% for three children, 31% for four children, and no less than 35% for five or more children The law that determines the amount of child support to be paid. tab > SA Ind field
