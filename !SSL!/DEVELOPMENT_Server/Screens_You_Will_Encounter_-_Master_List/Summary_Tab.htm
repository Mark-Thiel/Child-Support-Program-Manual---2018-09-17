Advanced Search Function screen

In ASSETS Automated State Support Enforcement and Tracking System (ASSETS)., go to:  Home > Modules > Inquiry > Advanced Search.

Note:  If you use WRTS Welfare Reporting and Tracking System. This is accessed through the Summary Tab.(Welfare Reporting and Tracking System) and SSN Social Security Number. to determine whether an NCP Non-Custodial Parent. Sometimes called the "absent parent." is on TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA)., this utility will tell you only whether or not NCP is on TA in New York State.
