Screens You'll Encounter in COLA

The main screens on ASSETS Automated State Support Enforcement and Tracking System (ASSETS). that a worker will encounter when working with COLA Cost-of-Living Adjustment, sometimes called a Cost-of-Living Allowance. are the:

  • Cost of Living Adjustment Eligible Cases screen

  • Cost of Living Adjustment (COLA) Information Edit screen

  • Child Support Standards Act (CSSA) Information Tab screen

  • Obligation Tab screen

  • Child Support Standards Act (CSSA) and Consumer Price Index Tables screen
