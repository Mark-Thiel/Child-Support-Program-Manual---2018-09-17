Account Creation Summary

In direct-from-court cases, the CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit worker In the Child Support Services Operations Manual, the term "worker" (in upper- or lower-case) refers to personnel working in any child support function. should retrieve the ACS Account Creation Summary. The ACS is an ASSETS-generated court report. It can provide info including the parties' and children's names, addresses, SSNs, DOBs, employment statuses, and name of NCP's employer. The ACS can be retrieved via the UCMS (Universal Case Management System)..  It contains a wealth of information useful in case-building. The Account Creation Summary can be accessed through the UCMS Universal Court Management System. This system pertains to Family Court only. Through the UCMS, workers can retrieve an ACS (Account Creation Summary) containing names, SSNs, DOBs, and much more..
