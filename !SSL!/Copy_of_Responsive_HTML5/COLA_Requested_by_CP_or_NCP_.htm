COLA Requested by CP or NCP?

 

Either a CP or an NCP can request a COLA.

 

Both will receive the "Notice of Your Right to Request a Cost of Living Adjustment to Your Child Support Order."

 

If neither party requests a COLA:

 

No further action is taken.

 

If CP or NCP wants to request a COLA:

 

Go to the next step:  "CP or NCP mails request to Processing Center."
