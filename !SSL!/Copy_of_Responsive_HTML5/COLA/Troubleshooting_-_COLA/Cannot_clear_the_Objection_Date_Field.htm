Cannot clear the Objection Date Field

 

If Adj. Order Effective Date passes while the Objection Date field is populated, Caseworker will be unable to clear the Objection Date field – meaning she can’t edit CEJ State field, and can’t adjust ledgers.

 

Caseworker will need to contact New York State Child Support Services to get Objection Date field cleared and, consequently, ledgers and CEJ State field unlocked.
