Ineligible Ledgers

 

 

 

Eligible ledgers may have different payment frequencies. For purposes of calculating a COLA, payment frequencies on all eligible ledgers will be normalized down to the frequency of the lowest common denominator/increment of time among them.

 

In the example above, all eligible ledgers would be normalized down to weekly amounts. The weekly amounts of all ledgers wold be summed, the COLA would be calculated based upon that sum, and then the COLA percentage would be applied proportionately to each eligible ledger.
