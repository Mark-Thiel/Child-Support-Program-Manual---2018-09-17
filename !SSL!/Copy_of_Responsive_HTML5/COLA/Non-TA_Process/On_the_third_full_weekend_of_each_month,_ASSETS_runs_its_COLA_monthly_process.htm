On the third full weekend of each month, ASSETS runs its COLA Cost of Living Allowance, also known as Cost of Living Adjustment monthly process

 

 

(Automated)

 

ASSETS does the following:

  • Identifies and normalizes all COLA-eligible ledgers
  • Calculates COLA percentage, COLA dollar amount, and adjusted support order obligation amount
  • Generates data files regarding all cases
