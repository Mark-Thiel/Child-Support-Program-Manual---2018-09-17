General Eligibility Criteria for a COLA

 

The amount of a child support obligation order can be increased administratively (i.e. without court involvement) by applying a COLA if:

 

1)    It has been at least two years since the support order was established, or since the obligation amount has been adjusted or modified,

 

and

 

2)    the sum of the percentage changes in CPI-Uâ€™s for the years since 1994, or since the original support order, or since the last year in which the order amount was adjusted or modified -- whichever is latest -- is equal to or greater than 10%.

 

When these two criteria are met, the case will appear on the Cost of Living Adjustment Eligible Cases screen.
