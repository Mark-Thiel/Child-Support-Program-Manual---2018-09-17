What This Manual Covers -- and What It Doesn't

What you are looking at here is a "first release" of the first chapter of the CSS Operations Manual.

Producing an Operations Manual serves a number of purposes, including (among others):

  • Capturing institutional knowledge that might otherwise be lost as seasoned personnel reach retirement age

  • Standardizing terminology

  • Standardizing procedures

  • Explaining processes that are otherwise invisible to the caseworker, capturing cause and effect within processes, giving insight into actions performed by the various actors in a process, and giving the end-user an appreciation of the big picture and his or her particular role in it

That said, it is recognized that there are 58 local districts in New York State, each with their own way of doing things.

The intention here is to publish a “first release” of the COLA chapter of the manual. The first release is meant to be limited in scope to that which is:

  • Clear and definitive

  • Universally true or applicable across all 58 local/county social service districts

  • Pragmatic information involving “typical” or “sunny day” scenarios encountered in everyday circumstances

As such, the first release of this chapter is not meant to be comprehensive. It is not meant to answer every question or cover every "what-if" scenario, error code, or contingency that may be encountered in the COLA process. (Those have been saved for a later release/update of the chapter.)

Based upon user feedback and other factors, this chapter may later be expanded or updated to include, for example, solutions to obstacles commonly encountered by child support workers on the front lines, as well as other issues.

The interface of this online Operations Manual has been designed to:

  1. Give end-users various ways to navigate the site and various ways to find information quickly

  2. Provide users with the information they request, when they request it, without inundating them with information that they did not request.

  3. Enable end-users to obtain further details or information about an issue very often without even having to leave the page that they are on. (Many of these features -- like pop-up screenshots, "Learn More" buttons, etc. -- are designed to be accessible to the end-user with a single click of the mouse.)

The manual is a work in progress. It is a large undertaking. The writer and designer of the manual is committed to quality assurance and quality improvement of this product.
