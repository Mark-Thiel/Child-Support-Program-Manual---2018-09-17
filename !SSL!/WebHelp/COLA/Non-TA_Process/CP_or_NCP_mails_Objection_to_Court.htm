CP or NCP Mails Objection to Court

CP Custodial Parent. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child. or NCP Non-Custodial Parent. Sometimes called the "absent parent." mails objection directly to court, and mails a copy to the other party (CP or NCP).
