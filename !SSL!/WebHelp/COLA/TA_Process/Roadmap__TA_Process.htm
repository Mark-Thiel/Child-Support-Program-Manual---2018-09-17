When you click a link in the map and are brought to drill-down content, how can you return to the map?

Three ways:

1)  Click "Non-TA Non-Temporary Assistance Process" in the table of contents.

2)  Click "Non-TA Process in the breadcrumbs.

3)  Click the "back" button on your browser.

 Also, if you scroll down to the bottom of the map and want to return to the top of the page, you can:

1)  Scroll back up to the top of the page, or

2)  Click the "Return to Top" button (it looks like an arrow, pointing up) in the bottom right of your screen.
