Worker enters Adjusted Order Objection Date into ASSETS

The worker In the Child Support Services Operations Manual, the term "worker" (in upper- or lower-case) refers to personnel working in any child support function. enters the objection date provided by the court into the Adj Order Objection Date field on the Obligation Tab screen.

(This is critical.  It locks the COLA Cost-of-Living Adjustment, sometimes called a Cost-of-Living Allowance.-affected ledgers so that they cannot be adjusted and a COLA cannot be applied).

Entering any value into the Adj. Order Objection Date field has the following effects:

  • Ledgers are locked so that they cannot be adjusted and COLA cannot be applied to them

  • CEJ Continuing Exclusive Jurisdiction. State field is locked so that it cannot be edited

(Note: Following determination of this matter in court, the Caseworker must clear the Adj Order Objection Date field, or else the ledgers and the CEJ State fields will remain locked.
