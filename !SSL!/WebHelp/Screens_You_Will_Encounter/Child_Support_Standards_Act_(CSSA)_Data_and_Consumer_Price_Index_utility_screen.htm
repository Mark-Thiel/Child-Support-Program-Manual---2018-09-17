Tables of the CPI-U The Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers (CPI-U) is a measure of the average change over time in the prices paid by urban consumers for a market basket of consumer goods and services. The figure is released by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. are also available at the website of the Bureau of Labor Statistics.

You can see those tables at:  http://www.bls.gov/cpi/tables.htm.
