normalized

Normalizing ledgers of different frequencies means breaking the various payment frequency cycles into their lowest common denominator to arrive at a common payment frequency. This is for purposes of applying percentage increases and making other calculations across disparate ledgers. It is a mechanism for \"compariing apples to apples\" when talking about disparate frequencies of payment.
