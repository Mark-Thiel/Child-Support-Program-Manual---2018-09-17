    TA Process
       Roadmap: TA Process
       Eligible cases appear on "Cost of Living Adjustment Eligible Cases" screen
       Worker reviews Eligible Cases screen, resolves any cases with Error codes
       ASSETS runs its COLA monthly process (on the third full weekend of each month)
       Proc Ctr mails "Adjusted Order of Support" to all parties
    Non-TA Process
       Roadmap: Non-TA Process Diagram
       Eligible cases appear on "Cost of Living Adjustment Eligible Cases" screen
       Worker reviews Eligible Cases screen, resolves any cases with Error codes
       ASSETS runs its COLA monthly process (on the third full weekend of each month)
       Processing Center prints and mails "Notice of Your Right to Request"
       No COLA requested? No further action.
       CP or NCP mails request to Processing Center
       Processing Center captures request for COLA
       ASSETS runs COLA monthly process (on the next third full weekend of the month
       Proc Ctr mails "Adjusted Order of Support" to all parties
       CP and NCP Decide Whether or Not to Object to COLA
       No objection? COLA takes effect in 60 days
       Objection: CP or NCP mails Objection to Court
