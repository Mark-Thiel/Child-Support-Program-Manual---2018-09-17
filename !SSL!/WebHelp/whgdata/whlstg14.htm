PA

Public Assistance. This is an old term for what is now called \"Temporary Assistance.\"

PEP

Paternity Establishment Percentage. This is one of five important performance measures (or KPIs -- Key Performance Indicators). The five are:  1) PEP (Paternity Establishment Percentage); 2) SEP (Support Establishment Percentage); 3) Collections on Current; 4) Collections on Arrears; and 5) Cost Effectiveness.
