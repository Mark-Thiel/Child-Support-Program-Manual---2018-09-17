CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit staff attempt to locate the NCP through inquiries at the last known address and place of employment, and through other means. Both State and Federal Parent Locator Services (PLS) are used. Each PLS conducts a computer match of absent parent names and SSNs with the files of state and federal agencies.

Addresses developed or obtained through searches are verified through use of an automatically-generated postal clearance letter.
