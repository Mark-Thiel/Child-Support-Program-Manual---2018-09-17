Group Codes

Group

Meaning

A

Account has been built.

L

Location efforts are ongoing. An L Group opens automatically if a case does not have a verified address for the NCP Non-Custodial Parent. Sometimes called the "absent parent.".

P

A P Group opens automatically if paternity has not yet been established.

X

Customer is active on TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA).

Y
