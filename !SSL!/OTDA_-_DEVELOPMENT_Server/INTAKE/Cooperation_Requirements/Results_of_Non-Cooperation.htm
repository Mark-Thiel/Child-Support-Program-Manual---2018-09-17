Results of Non-Cooperation with the Child Support Program

When an applicant in a TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA). or MA referral does not meet requirements of cooperating with the CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit, the CSEU notifies the TA or MA Unit of this via form LDSS-2859 Information Transmittal Form. (See Mary Hans first packet for photochopy of this form). This form is by child support, temporary assistance (TA), foster care (FC) and other programs to transmit case information. It is also used by Medicaid (MA) to receive child support information..

The TA Unit may then sanction the client.

What does "sanction" mean?

It means that the TA or MA benefits may be reduced by 25% until such time as CSEU notifies the TA or MA Unit that the client is now in complance with the requirement to cooperate with the CSEU.
