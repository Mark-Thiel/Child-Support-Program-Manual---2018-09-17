Define "Non-Cooperation" with the Child Support Program

Every client has the right to attest that the information required about the NCP Non-Custodial Parent. Sometimes called the "absent parent." cannot be produced.

When a client claims to have lack of information, form LDSS-4281 -- or its Spanish equivalent, form LDSS-4281S -- must be completed and signed by the applicant.
