Section C:  Checking Box 1 or Box 2 -- or Both

Note: For an application or request (including petition, motion, etc.) for child support services to be actionable by the CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit, it must be signed by the individual (CP Custodial Parent. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child. or NCP) requesting services. Cases should not be built unless and until such a signed document is received and on file at the CSEU.

Signatures may be captured electronically, using an electronic signature pad. In this instance, the page bearing the signature of the customer must be printed out and kept on file in the CSEU. The document must be readily available in event of an audit.

Box 1

Box 1 should be checked and signed by:

  • Non-TA Non-Temporary Assistance applicants who are not required to cooperate with the child support program, but are applying of their own volition

  • TA or MA referrals who have checked and signed Box 2 (as required) and also wish to pursue child support services even if their temporary assistance application is denied (i.e. even if the cooperation requirement is nullified)

Box 2

Box 2 should be checked and signed by:

Applicants for temporary assistance (TA or MA). They are required to cooperate with the child support program as a prerequisite for receiving TA or MA benefiits.
