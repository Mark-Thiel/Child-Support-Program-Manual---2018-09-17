CP requests continuation of child support services following discontinuance of TA assistance

In TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA). cases where the ROS Recipient of Services. This may be a CP receiving child support services, or it may be an NCP receiving paternity establishment services or other services. In some cases, both CP and NCP may be receiving services. has received a waiver for good cause or for domestic violence.

If the TA Unit closes the TA case, and the customer wishes to continue receiving child support services, the customer must, within 30 days, write and sign a letter to the CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit wherein the customer clearly requests child support services with a phrase such as "I want you to begin or resume child support services."

If no such letter is received by the CSEU within 30 days of the discontinuation of TA benefits, the CSEU will close the case.

This is explained to the CP Custodial Parent. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child. in a Continuation of Child Support Services letter that is sent to them automatically upon closing of the TA case by the TA.
