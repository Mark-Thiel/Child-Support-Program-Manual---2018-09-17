If the requirement to cooperate changes after a TA/MA referral to the CSEU

In a TA case, if a customer:

  • checks only Box 2 in Section C of the LDSS-4882,

AND

  • is subsequently found ineligible for TA or MA,

AND

  • the customer stills seeks child support services,

an application for such services must be made pursuant to SSL § 111-g.  

However, if the TA or MA case was closed less than 30 days prior, rather than filling out a new LDSS-4882, the applicant may revise the previously submitted LDSS-4882.

To do this, the applicant must:

      1. Check Box 1 in Section C – Application/Affirmation for Child Support Services;

      2. Complete the associated signature area; and

      3. Initial the changes to mark the LDSS-4882 as having been amended

(Note that the worker should have asked the TA referral customer during the original intake interview whether he/she wants to pursue child support services even in the event of denial of temporary assistance. If so, the customer should have checked off and signed both Box 1 and Box 2 in Section C of the LDSS-4882.)
