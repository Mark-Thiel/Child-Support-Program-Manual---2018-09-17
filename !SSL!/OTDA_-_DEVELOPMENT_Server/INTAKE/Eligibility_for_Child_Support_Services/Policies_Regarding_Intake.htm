Equal Access to Services

TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA). and Non-TA Non-Temporary Assistance clients are entitled to the same range of services.

Non-TA cases are entitled to receive, on an equal basis, all child support services that are available for TA cases.  Non-TA applicants are to be advised of:

  • all services available to them (location, paternity establishment, support establishment, collection, and enforcement)

  • the distribution policy

  • the mandatory $25 annual federal CP Custodial Parent. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child. fee

  • costs associated with any legal representation provided in Non-TA cases [18NYCRR 347.17]
