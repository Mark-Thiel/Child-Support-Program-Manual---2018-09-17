Do ALL customers need to fill out an LDSS-4882?

No.

The following types of clients do not need to fill out an LDSS-4882 "Information about Child Support Services and "Application/Referral for Child Support Services" intake form. (Form LDSS-4882) (This form has replaced form DSS-2860.):

  • Direct-from-court cases in which a court order is sent from court to the CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit, and is accompanied by a petition, application, or motion Â for child support services signed by the applicant.

  • Direct-from-court cases in which customer has already applied for child support services in another SSD Social Service District or state.

In some districts, applications are filled out online by the caseworker during the intake interview. In those instances, the required signature can be captured using an electronic signature pad, or the worker In the Child Support Services Operations Manual, the term "worker" (in upper- or lower-case) refers to personnel working in any child support function. can print out the completed application and have the applicant sign it.

Regardless of how the signature is captured, the page bearing the signed request for services must be kept on file, in hard copy, and must be readily available at any time in the event of an audit.
