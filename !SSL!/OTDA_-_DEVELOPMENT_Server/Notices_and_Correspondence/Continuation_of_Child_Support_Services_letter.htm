Continuation of Child Support Services letter

Note: Customers who received a waiver from child support cooperation due to good cause or domestic violence will NOT have their cases automatically continued upon termination of TA Temporary Assistance. "Temporary Assistance" includes Family Assistance (FA) and Safety Net Assistance (SNA). services. (See third paragraph, below.) These customers must explicitly request continuation of services in writing, within 30 days of the discontinuation of TA services, or their child support services will cease.
