CP or NCP mails request to Processing Center

Either party can request a COLA Cost-of-Living Adjustment, sometimes called a Cost-of-Living Allowance. by filling out the Request for a Cost of Living Adjustment (COLA) form and mailing it back to the Processing Center. (The CP Custodial Parent. This is the parent with whom the child is living. However, the "Custodial Parent" could be a guardian such as a grandparent, foster home, or other person or entity with whom the child is living, and who is taking care of the child. and NCP are provided a self-addressed, postpaid envelope for this purpose.)

There is no deadline for mailing in the form.
