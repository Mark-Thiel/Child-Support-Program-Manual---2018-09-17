Eligible Ledgers

Any given account may contain some ledgers that are subject to COLA Cost-of-Living Adjustment, sometimes called a Cost-of-Living Allowance. increases and some ledgers that are not.

ASSETS Automated State Support Enforcement and Tracking System (ASSETS). displays on the Cost of Living Adjustment Eligible Cases screen all accounts that contain COLA-eligible ledgers, even if an account contains a mix of eligible and ineligible ledgers.

There are a total of 11 types of ledgers that are eligible for COLA increases. They are:

Ineligible Ledgers

When an account contains some eligible and some ineligible ledgers, the ineligible ledgers do not render the entirety of the account ineligible for a COLA increase. However, only the eligible ledgers will be factored in to the calculations of the COLA.
