Introduction to Support Collection

Source: 03 ADM 5

Once an order of support has been established in court, the CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit builds/establishes an account reflecting the amount of the support obligation and the frequency of payments required by the order.

If we have a verified employer address for the NCP Non-Custodial Parent. Sometimes called the "absent parent.", ASSETS Automated State Support Enforcement and Tracking System (ASSETS). will automatically send an IWO Income Withholding Order. Also known as "income execution." IWOs are sent to employers in an automated administrative process that deducts support payments from an NCP's wages or other income, including unemployment benefits. to the NCP's employer. If there is no verified employer address, an IWO cannot be issued.  Instead, a computer-generated billing statement is mailed to the NCP, advising the dates and amounts that scheduled payments are due.
