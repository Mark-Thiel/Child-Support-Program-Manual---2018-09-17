Lump Sum Payments

If an employer makes a lump sum payment to an NCP Non-Custodial Parent. Sometimes called the "absent parent.", they should report it to the CSS Child Support Services..

This can be done through the web portal, or they can phone the CSEU Child Support Enforcement Unit to inform them of the lump sum payment.

Examples of lump sum payments might be:

  • Severance pay

  • One-time bonus
