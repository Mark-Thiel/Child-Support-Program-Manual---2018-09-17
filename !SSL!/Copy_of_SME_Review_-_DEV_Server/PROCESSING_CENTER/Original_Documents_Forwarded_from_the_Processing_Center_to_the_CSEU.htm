Originals of Documents Forwarded from the Processing Center to the CSEU

Some documents that are mailed to the Processing Center are forwarded on to the CSEU in original, hard-copy form (versus electronic copies available via ECS). 

(The Processing Center still scans them, and retains a digital copy.)

??MT - Question:  With these docs, in addition to the originals being mailed from the Processing Center to the CSEU, are digital copies also available via ECS?

The following types of documents, when received at the Processing Center, are sent to the CSEU in original, hard-copy form:

    • Notarized letters/forms

    • DMV affidavit/Affidavit of Net Worth

    • Tax Offset Responses

    • Medical cards or notices of insurance
