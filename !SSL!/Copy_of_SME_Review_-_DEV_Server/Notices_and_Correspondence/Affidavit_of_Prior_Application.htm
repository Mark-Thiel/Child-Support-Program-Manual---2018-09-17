RE:   No Child Support Application on File with Support Collection Unit

Dear (Recipient of Support Payments):

This Support Collection Unit has identified that there is no application for child support services in your case record.  Federal regulations require all states to provide child support services to an individual in a never-assistance case where such individual files an application requesting child support services.  

We have reviewed the case record for the:

 order of support entered by the __________  Supreme Court  Family Court which directed that all payments be made to this Support Collection Unit.

 notice issued by the Support Collection Unit to redirect all payment under the order of support entered by the _________ court to this Support Collection Unit.

The case record does not have the required application for child support services.  Therefore, you must take immediate action as identified below in order for us to continue providing child support services to you for your order of support.

If you applied for child support services directly through the Family Court or Supreme Court, you must provide this Support Collection Unit with a copy of the petition, application, or motion for support that resulted in the order of support.  The copy of the petition, application, or motion must include a statement signed by you that clearly indicates you were applying for child support services.  

If you cannot provide a copy of the petition, application, or motion for child support services which includes a statement signed by you that clearly indicates you previously applied for child support services, you must complete and return the Application/Referral for Child Support Services section in the enclosed LDSS-4882, Information about Child Support Services and Application/Referral for Child Support Services.  The Application/Referral for Child Support Services section is found on pages A-1 through A-8 of the LDSS-4882 and may be detached in order to complete the form.  You must sign Box 1 on page A-8.  You may keep the Information about Child Support Services section found on pages 1 through 7 of the LDSS-4882 for your records.  

In addition, an Affidavit of Prior Application for Child Support Services is enclosed for completion.  Please refer to the information in the box checked below for instructions for this form:

  If you believe that you previously submitted an application for child support services to this Support Collection Unit handling your case, or if you believe that you or your attorney previously submitted a petition, application, or motion to the court which included a statement signed by you that clearly indicated that you applied for child support services, along with completion of the LDSS-4882 mentioned above, you must also complete the enclosed Affidavit of Prior Application for Child Support Services indicating that you previously applied for child support services.  The Affidavit of Prior Application for Child Support Services must be signed and dated in front of a notary public.  However, if you do not believe that you previously requested child support services from this Support Collection Unit, do not complete the Affidavit of Prior Application for Child Support Services.

 Although the case record does not have the required application for child support services currently being provided, records for your case indicate that you did apply for child support services from this Support Collection Unit on or about  ________ .  As such, along with completion of the LDSS-4882 mentioned above, you must also complete the enclosed Affidavit of Prior Application for Child Support Services indicating that you previously applied for child support services.  You must sign and date the Affidavit of Prior Application for Child Support Services in front of a notary public.   

You must return either: (1) a copy of the petition, application, or motion which includes a statement signed by you that clearly indicates you applied for child support services, or (2) the completed and signed Application/Referral for Child Support Services section (pages A1 through A8) of the LDSS-4882, along with the completed, signed, and notarized Affidavit of Prior Application for Child Support Services, if appropriate as described above, within fifteen (15) calendar days from the date of this letter to:

 

If you do not return the necessary documents identified above within fifteen (15) calendar days of the date of this letter, this Support Collection Unit will be required to discontinue providing child support services and close your case.
