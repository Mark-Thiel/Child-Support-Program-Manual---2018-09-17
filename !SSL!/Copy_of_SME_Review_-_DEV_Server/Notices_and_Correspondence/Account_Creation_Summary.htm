What information can the Account Creation Summary provide?

The ACS, an ASSETS-generated [family] court report, can provide information regarding:

    • Court order date; court action; type of obligation

    • Court information, including support magistrate and location

    • Obligation amount and frequency; how custodial parents will receive payments

    • Names of the parties/parents

    • Addresses of the parties

    • SSNs of the parties

    • Dates of birth of the parties and children

    • Each party's employment status and income

    • Name of the NCP's employer, if employed

    • Names of the children

    • Medical insurance

In direct-from-court cases, the Account Creation Summary (ACS) Report should be retrieved or the UCMS inquiry function accessed in order to obtain the information for case-building.

(If the ACS is not available, information about the parties can be found by viewing the roster information on the Court Inquiry page of the UCMS inquiry function.)

For instructions on how to retrieve the ACS Family Court report or view roster information on the Court Inquiry page of the UCMS inquiry function on ASSETS, refer to the ASSETS User Guide.
