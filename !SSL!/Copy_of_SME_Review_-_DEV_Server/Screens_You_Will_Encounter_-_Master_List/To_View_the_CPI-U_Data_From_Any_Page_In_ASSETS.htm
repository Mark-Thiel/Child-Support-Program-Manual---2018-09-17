To View the CPI-U Data From Any Page In ASSETS:

Look on the Child Support Standards Act (CSSA) and Consumer Price Index Tables screen.

In ASSETS, go to: Home screen > Utilities > CSSA/CPI

Tables of the CPI-U are also available at the website of the Bureau of Labor Statistics (http://www.bls.gov/cpi/tables.htm).
