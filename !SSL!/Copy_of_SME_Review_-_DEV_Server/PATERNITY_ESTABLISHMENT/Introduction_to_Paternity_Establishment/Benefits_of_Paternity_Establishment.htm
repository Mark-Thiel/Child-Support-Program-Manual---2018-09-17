Benefits of Paternity Establishment

The benefits of establishing paternity go beyond financial concerns.

Benefits for the Child:

    • Legal record of parents' identity

    • Father's name on child's birth certificate

    • Information on family medical history

    • Emotional benefits of knowing who both parents are

    • Financial support from parents, including child support, social security, veterans' benefits, and inheritance

    • Medical or life insurance from either parent, if available

Benefits for the Mother:

    • Help in sharing parental responsibilities

    • Information about the father's medical history

    • Improving financial security for the family

    • Access to medical coverage for the child from the father's medical insurance, if available

Benefits for the Father:

    • Legal establishment of parental rights

    • Father's name on child's birth certificate

    • Right to seek court-ordered custody and visitation rights

    • Right to be informed and have a say in adoption proceedings

    • Protection of rights if the mother becomes unable to care for the child
