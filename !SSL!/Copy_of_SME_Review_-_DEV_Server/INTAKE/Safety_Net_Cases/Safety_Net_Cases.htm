Safety Net Cases

Safety Net is similar to TANF, but is not the same as TANF.

  • The Commissioner or Designee of the social services district — as the applicant for child support services — must print his/her name, sign the form, and enter the date under Box 1 of page A-8 of the LDSS-4882 form.

  • The TA worker must complete the "Agency Use Only" section at the bottom of page A-8.

Note:  Date of Referral is the date that the CAN is added to the database.

  • When a Safety Net case is built, ASSETS automatically opens a Y Group.
