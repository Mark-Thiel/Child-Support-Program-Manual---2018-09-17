Referral to Domestic Violence Liaison

Source:  12-ADM-03

If a customer indicates a safety concern on the LDSS-4882, the worker must stop the intake interview and refer the customer — following local protocols  — to a Domestic Violence Liaison for screening and assessment.

In TA cases, the CSEU must notify the TA Unit of this action via the LDSS-2859.

All CSEU activities must be suspended during the domestic violence screening and assessment process, and until a waiver decision is made by the Domestic Violence Liaison.
