If the objection is upheld

If the objection to the COLA is upheld, the Adjusted Order of Support issued to the CP and NCP must be rescinded:

    • The worker clears the Adj. Order Objection Date field.

    • The worker rescinds the Adjusted Order of Support by entering Code "R" (rescind) in the COLA Request Indicator field on the Cost of Living Adjustment (COLA) Information Edit screen.

The Court Order Date is the earlier of the date of the court determination on the Obligation Tab screen or the Adj Order Effective Date on the COLA Tab screen (i.e., 60 days from the date the adjusted order was mailed).
