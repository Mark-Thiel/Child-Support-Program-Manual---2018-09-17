No COLA requested? No further action required.

In a Non-TA case, if neither the CP nor the NCP requests a COLA, the COLA Request Ind (Indicator) field will remain blank, and no further action will be taken.

Two years later, if the case is still eligible for a COLA, both parties will again be sent notice that it is eligible.

The COLA will be recalculated by ASSETS at that time, and the notice sent to the CP and NCP will have updated figures regarding the percentage and dollar amount of the proposed COLA.
