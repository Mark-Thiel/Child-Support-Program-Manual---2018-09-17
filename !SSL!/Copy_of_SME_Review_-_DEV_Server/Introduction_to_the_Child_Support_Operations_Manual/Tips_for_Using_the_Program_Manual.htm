Tips for Using the Program Manual

 

Find Status and Reason Codes — Fast!

Searching for the meaning of a specific Status and Reason Code? Just enter the code itself into the Search box, and hit enter.

Examples:

    • V802 > Enter

    • L777 > Enter

    • V001 > Enter

Find Forms and Documents — Fast!

Just enter the number the form into the search box, and hit Enter. There's usually no need to enter the DSS or LDSS prefix.  For example, to find form LDSS-2859, just enter "2859" into the Search box, and hit Enter.
