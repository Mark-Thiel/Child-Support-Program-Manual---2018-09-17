Laws and Regulations Related to Support Establishment

Source: CS101 Manual

Law or Regulation

Description

Social Services Law (SSL) 111-h

This is a state mandate that supports a federal regulation that requires a centralized location for the collection and disbursement of child support payments.

18 New York Codes, Rules, and Regulations ยง346.1

This state regulation gives authority to the local district SCU to provide for the collection, accounting, and disbursement of child support payments.

Family Court Act (FCA) 440 1(b)(1)

Provides for court-ordered child support to be paid through the SCU.

CPLR 5241

Gives the local district CSEU the authoirty to issue an immediate Income WIthholding Order (IWO) when the court issues a child support order.
