Introduction to Child Support

Based upon Onandaga:  http://www.ongov.net/dss/childSupport.html

The Office of Child Support assists custodial parents in seeking court-ordered child support for their children.

The program will establish paternity, as well as obtain and enforce medical support orders. Once orders are issued by the family court or supreme court, accounts are set up, payments are monitored, and enforcement actions take place when no payment is received.

CSEUs provide many services to the residents of their counties. Some of these include:

  • Locating absent parents

  • Paternity and support establishment

  • Enforcement of support orders through driver's license suspension, property execution, income execution, passport denial, and various other tools
