Local Resources for Location Investigation — a Checklist

Source: Mike Maynard's list of search methods used in regard to Undistributed Collections

NOTE:  The checklist below will be replaced by a checklist provided by Policy.  (Awaiting checklist)

  1.   Conduct a statewide name and SSN search via the Advanced Search screen in ASSETS

  2.   If needed, re-interview the customer to obtain more information about the Respondent

  3.   Generate a PCL for Respondent's last known address

  1.   Generate a Wage and Health Benefits Report for Respondent's last known employer

  1.   Conduct an internet search of:

      1.  County assessments

      2.  Tax rolls

      3.  WhitePages.com

      4.  ZABA Search

      5.  Civil lawsuits

      6.  Criminal actions

  1.   Veterans organizations — Refer to OCSE DCL-13-25 and attachments

  1.  Prison/inmate, parole, and probation records

  1.   Local online newspapers:

      1.  Obituaries

      2. Real estate transactions

      3. Police records

      4. Court records

  1.   If a search turns up arrest records, search:

      1. VINELink — Search state prisons in states other than New York

      2.  Department of Corrections

      3.  Parole and/or Probation Officer

  1.   Review Family Court and Supreme Court files for:

      1.  Social Security number

      2.  Last known address

      3.  Employer leads

      4.  Other identifying information
