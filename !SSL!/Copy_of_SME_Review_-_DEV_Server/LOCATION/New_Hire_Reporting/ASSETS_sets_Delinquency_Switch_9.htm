ASSETS sets Delinquency Switch 9

If the employer does not respond to an IWO within 45 days:

    1. ASSETS sets Delinquency Switch 9.

    2. The worker sees Delinquency Switch 9 on the Worker Alerts > Enforcement Mode > Delinq. Switches tab.

    3. The worker clicks on Delinquency Switch messages to learn required actions.
