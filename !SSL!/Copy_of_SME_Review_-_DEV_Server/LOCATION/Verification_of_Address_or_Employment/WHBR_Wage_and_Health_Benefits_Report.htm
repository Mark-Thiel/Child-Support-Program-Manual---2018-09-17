Wage and Health Benefits Report (WHBR)

All employer information must be verified via a WHBR.

How to manually generate a WHBR

When a worker manually enters a new employer record into the case record in ASSETS, generating a WHBR to verify that employer is elected as an option in the course of that procedure.

To see a description of that procedure, see Manually Enter a PF/NCP Employer Into the Case Record.

 

New employer data obtained from NDNH or SDNH? WHBR is issued automatically. (Case level only.)

If employer information is found through automated searches of the National Directory of New Hires (NDNH) or the State Directory of New Hires (SDNH):

    1. ASSETS or the Processing Center automatically enters a Code V into the Employer Verification field on the Case Mode > Resp Emp tab;

    2. ASSETS automatically generates a WHBR .

    3. The Processing Center prints it and mails the WHBR to the employer.
