Military

A search of the Department of Defense (DOD) database is included in automated PLS searches. Search hits are reported on the Worker Alerts > Enforcement Mode > PLS tab, and data found auto-populates the case record.

Information found is recorded on the Case Mode > Location tab, the Case Mode > Resp Emp tab, and the Summary Mode > Resp Addr tab.

The worker can conduct a manual search of the DOD Entitlement and/or Locate tabs on the child support portal, which is accessible via the ASSETS home screen under Quick Links > FPLS State Services Portal.
