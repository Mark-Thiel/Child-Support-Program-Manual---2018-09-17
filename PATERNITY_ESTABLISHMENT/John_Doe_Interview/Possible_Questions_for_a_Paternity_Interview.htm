Possible Questions for a Paternity Interview

Source:  All questions are from the 1989 child support manual

 

  1. If the child is not yet born, what is the expected birth date?

  2. Do you have the child's birth certificate?

  3. What is the child DOB?

Was the pregnancy full term for this child?

What was the weight of the child at birth?

Did the child have any hair? Fingernails or toenails?  Eyebrows?

  1. Where was the child born?

Please provide name and address of facility.

  1. Do you have any records from the facility, or statements from a physician pertaining to your hospitalization for your pregnancy and the birth of your child?

  2. Have the bills for medical costs (hospital, doctors, etc.) been paid? By whom?

  3. If you were hospitalized for, or as a result of, the birth of a child, did the putative father visit you or the child during that time? When?

Did the putative father bring any gifts to the child at birth? What was it? When did he give it to the child?

  1. Has the putative father ever given any gifts to the child?

  1. Did you have sexual intercourse with anyone other than the person you named as the father within 45 days prior to or after the date of conception?

Please provide name(s) and address(es).

  1. Is there any other information concerning your relationship with the putative father that you can provide?

 

  1. When did you meet the putative father?

Where did you meet him?

  1. When did you begin dating him?

When did you stop seeing him?

How often or how many times did you see him between these times?

  1. Have you ever lived with the putative father?

Where and when did you live with him?

How long did you live with him?

  1. Did you ever register with the putative father at a hotel, motel, or other establishment or event (bank, wedding reception, etc.)?

Where and when?

  1. When did you first have sexual intercourse with the putative father?

Where did this take place?

  1. What was the date that you became pregnant?

Why do you believe that this date is correct?

What was the date of your last menstrual period before this pregnancy?

Are your menstrual periods regular?

Do you have any records or statements from a physician or medical facility identifying the date of conception?

  1. Beginning from the point of 60 days before you became pregnant, how often did you have sexual intercourse with the putative father?

Where did this take place?

Was anyone else present in the house/apartment/location at any of these times?

(Please provide names, addresses, and relationships.)

  1. Did you or the putative father use any contraceptive methods?

If so, which one(s)?  With what regularity?

  1. When did you tell the putative father that you were pregnant?

Where and how did you tell him?

What was his response?  (Are there any texts, or things of that nature?)

  1. Have you ever discussed your pregnancy with the putative father in the presence of anyone else?

If so, please provide: name, address, relationship, date and time of discussion, and location of discussion.

 

  1. Has the putative father ever stated, mentioned, or admitted that he is the father of the child?

Verbally or in writing?

To whom?

When?

If verbally, who was present at the time?  (names and addresses)

  1. Are there any friends, neighbors, landlords, etc., who have seen the mother with the putative father?

Please provide names, addresses, relationships, where the meeting took place, when it took place, and the circumstances under which it took place.
