  1. What is a Paternity Petition?

    A Paternity Petition is a formal, written application to a court requesting judicial action to determine legal fatherhood of a specific man for a specific child. The Paternity Petition asks the Family Court to issue an Order of Filiation for the child in the case.

  3. How to prepare a Paternity Petition

    1. In ASSETS, go to:  Home > Modules > Document Generation > NYS Family Court tab

    1. Select the NYS Family Court tab.

    1. Select the Petitioner:

      • For TA cases, select Petitioner: Commissioner of Social Services, Assignee (DSS)

      • For Non-TA cases, select Petitioner: Child Support Services Applicant (CSS)

    1. Select Petition Type: Paternity Petition

    1. Select Filing Method: Paper Petition Filing or Electronic Petition Filing

    2. Select Include Documents: Personal Information Form (if required).

    1. If the customer has requested address confidentiality on the Personal Information Form, select Address Confidentiality Affidavit, as well.

    The Address Confidentiality Affidavit must be completed by the customer.

    1. Click the Continue button.

    The worker uses information provided on the LDSS-4882 and Affidavit Alleging Paternity to fill in fields on the petition.

    Datafields in the Personal Information Form will be auto-populated with information supplied by ASSETS.
